About Me!

I am a junior Elementary Education major at the University of Vermont. I chose the topic of yoga because I have recently become very interested in it and wanted to learn more! Specifically why it is so beneficial.

I am currently a beginner who uses yoga to improve my flexibility and reduce stress. My goal is to get much better at it and maybe one day bring these practices into my future classroom!

I recently mastered my headstand in one of the easy variations and I am excited to get stronger and hopefully try a harder one soon!
