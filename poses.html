Common Yoga Poses and Their Benefits

Pose Name Pose Image Benefit Difficulty
Child's Pose Relieves stress and anxiety. Also great for opening up your hips. Beginner
Downward Dog Strengthens the arms and legs, while also stretching the hamstrings and calves. Beginner
Headstand Improves balance and coordination. Also helps to build strength in the arms, core, and shoulders. There are many variations to the headstand. Advanced
Warrior III Strengthens the legs and core, while also improving balance and stability. Intermediate
Lotus Pose Opens the hips and stretches the ankles. Also helps to improve balance and focus. Intermediate to Advanced
